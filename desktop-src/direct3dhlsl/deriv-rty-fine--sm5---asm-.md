| Deriv \_ propriété \_ fine \[ \_ SAT \] dest \[ . Mask \] , \[ - \] src0 \[ \_ ABS \] \[ . Swizzle \] , |
|--------------------------------------------------------------------------|



 



| Élément                                                            | Description                                                    |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|
